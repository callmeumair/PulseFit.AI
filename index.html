Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PulseFit AI

What is PulseFit AI?

PulseFit AI is an innovative fitness platform that combines artificial intelligence with personalized nutrition and fitness tracking. It helps users achieve their health goals through AI-powered meal planning, progress tracking, and personalized recommendations. Our platform adapts to your unique needs, preferences, and progress to provide the most effective guidance for your fitness journey.

How does PulseFit AI work?

PulseFit AI uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze your fitness goals, dietary preferences, and progress data to create personalized nutrition plans and track your fitness journey. Our AI adapts to your needs and provides real-time recommendations.

What makes PulseFit AI different from other fitness apps?

Unlike traditional fitness apps, PulseFit AI provides truly personalized recommendations that evolve with your progress. Our AI learns from your data and adjusts your plans in real-time, ensuring you always get the most effective guidance for your fitness journey.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee, and you can cancel your subscription through your account settings without any hidden fees or penalties.

How secure is my personal data?

We take data security seriously. All your personal information and fitness data are encrypted and stored securely. We comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations, and we never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer different levels of support based on your plan. Free users get access to our knowledge base and community support. Pro users receive priority email support, while Customizable plan users get 1-on-1 support and dedicated account management.

Can I use PulseFit AI without a smartphone?

Yes! PulseFit AI is accessible through any web browser, so you can use it on your computer, tablet, or smartphone. We also offer a mobile app for iOS and Android for a more convenient experience on the go.
